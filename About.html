JAGRUTI VIKAS BAHUUDDESHIYA SWANS

JAGRUTI VIKAS BAHUUDDESHIYA SWANS orgnisation is an NGO in India bassed in Goregaon Mumbai.The NGO Registerd in the Year 2019 Under bombai Public Trusts Act-1950(Act number 29 of bombay public trust act 1950) And Societies Registration Act,1860. The Primary objective of JAGRUTI VIKAS BAHUUDDESHIYA SWANS to provide help to economically backward people for their Education and health and Provide food to needy People

JVBS continuously Serving for people welfare in different sector to empower and Support helpless People. we belive that a small contribution can have impactful and positive Changes many people have join our initiative to help needy people .
