JAGRUTI VIKAS BAHUUDDESHIYA SWANS's Food and Ration distribution Program

We aims that Not single human should go hungry our efforts are always that we can help more needy and hungry people through Our NGO. Every day During Covid-19 time our NGO distributed food and grains Among needy people. During Covid time , it was always our endeavor that no family starved in this crisis . Due to lockdown Many companies and Other source of income had been Stoped due to which people faced huge financial problem .many family Could not even gather two meals of day. After Looking at challenges Our NGO took Initiative to Distribute food and Ration Among Needy people
